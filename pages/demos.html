01

Automated EV Battery Disassembly & Second-Life Sorting

The Challenge

The world faces a tsunami of used EV batteries. Manual disassembly is slow, dangerous due to high voltages, and economically inefficient. The battery recycling market is projected to grow from $11B to $53B by 2030.

Our Solution

Unlike assembly, which is repetitive, disassembly of used products is chaotic. No two battery packs arrive in the same condition. Our cognitive robotics system can perceive, understand, plan, and act on unpredictable environments.

5x Faster Processing
100% Safety Compliance

One-Shot Defect Detection & Robotic Marking

Revolutionary Approach

Show the system one example of a new defect. The AI learns to not only identify this defect on other parts but also to pinpoint its exact location. Coordinates are sent to a robot arm for immediate marking.

Impact

10x improvement in manufacturing inspection by moving from passive defect detection to immediate, automated, physical action. This showcases a tangible step towards fully autonomous, high-variability production.
